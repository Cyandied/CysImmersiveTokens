My story

I play DnD and I am a DM! My favorite way of enjoying DnD and other PnP rpgs is through the use of virtual table-tops, my favorite is Foundry VTT! It offers a million things in ways of making the game more accessable for new players (doing all the math for you) and automates tedious bookkeeping that would otherwise slow the game down! For that reason, I want to fully utilize the options that a virtual enviroment gives me, I want to add as much immersion as I possibly can! So I spend countless hours finding maps, tokens, plug-ins, and sounds to up my game and make it an experience my players will cherish. Thats how this projects started! I wanted tokens that are easy and fast to make, with a wide option for cotumizability, so that I can easily make unique tokens for any given situation. My players find a ginat emerald, but all I have are generic gem tokens? 1 minute tops in the editor, and I have a green square cut emerald (like from minecraft) to put in my players' inventory! While you might not think these things are that important, I do, and I wish to share my little creation with the world! So now it is your problem!
